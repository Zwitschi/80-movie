Open Mic Odyssey: The Movie

{{ movie_description }}

This independent documentary trailer introduces the comedy road trip documentary journey at the center of Open Mic Odyssey. Follow a group of friends as they embark on an outrageous adventure.

The film follows aspiring stand-up comedians as they hit the road for a cross-country tour in a self-driving Tesla, performing at various open mic nights and long drives in between. This documentary explores the dedication, the heartbreak, the camaraderie, and the undeniable passion required to chase a dream that most people consider crazy, and gives an intimate look at the world of stand-up comedy and three friends supporting each other on their journey to make a creative life real. The road to success is paved with bad jokes, tough crowds, and endless miles.
